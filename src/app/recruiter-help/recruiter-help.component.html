Account Management

Your Account

Making another user the Account Admin.
Open the Toolkit and select ‘Your Admin’. From there select ‘Manage Settings’, on this page you can see a list of all users, simply press the ‘Make Admin’ button next to the user you wish to make the account administrator.

Adding other users to your account
You can add users to your account easily. Go to ‘My Home’ and click ‘Add Another Account’. From here you can purchase more users on your account.

Your Profile

Your Profile is the place to put all of your Company details.
Adding a profile photo gives your company a face on ContractRecruit, just click Upload, and use the file browser to choose your desired picture

You can enter your company details, including location on this page. If you wish to add more than one office address then you can select ‘Add Another Address’.

Company Social
RSS Feed – If you have an RSS Feed you can enter it here. This will display publicly on your profile.
Twitter Feed – The Twitter feed displays your company’s latest Tweets from your Twitter account. Please note in ‘Twitter Account Name’ field you should enter your Twitter name, not the URL. For example you should enter: @BBCNewsuk not: twitter.com/BBCNewsuk

Manage Settings

This is the section where you can review the consultants on your account, your password, and your payment package.

Manage Consultants – When you have multiple users on your account you can manage them here. If you wish to change the Account Administrator it can be done here also.

If you wish to change your password it can be done on this page. Enter your old password, followed by your new password, and you’re done. The next time you login you can use your new password, so make sure you remember that you changed it!
