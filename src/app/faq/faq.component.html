General

What is ContractRecruit?

ContractRecruit is a unique job board, specialising solely in IT Contract positions. Our site contains features unlike any other, designed with the Contractor in mind, to make the search for your next Contract easier than ever before.

I’ve forgotten my password, how do I log in?

Please select the ‘Forgot Your Password’ link underneath the login module, then enter your email address and we will send you an email allowing you to reset your password. Select the link in the email and follow the instructions. Still having problems? Please get in touch.

How can I give ContractRecruit Feedback?

We really appreciate feedback at ContractRecruit and anything that can make our service better for you we’d like to hear about! On most pages you’ll find a feedback tab on the left hand side of the page, please fill it out and send it off, we’ll do our utmost to act on your suggestions however big or small!
